Frequently Asked Questions

How should I take care of my chambira bag?

Take care of it by avoiding prolonged exposure to sunlight and storing it in places free of dust and humidity.

Can I wash my bag or sandals?

You can clean them with a soft sponge and liquid soap, gently scrubbing and then rinsing well. Let them dry in the shade.

How can I take care of my rubber items?

Avoid getting them dirty with grease or alcohol-based liquids, and try to clean them at least once a month using a few drops of unsalted natural oil, rubbing from the center to the edges.

Can I request custom orders?

We love creating custom pieces. Please contact us with your ideas and requirements.

Do you offer home delivery and nationwide shipping?
