Our Workshops

At Shaju, we believe in sharing our knowledge and passion for traditional crafts. Our workshops are designed for all skill levels and provide a supportive environment to learn and create.

What to Expect:

  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • All materials and tools provided
  • Guidance from experienced artisans
  • Take home your handcrafted creation
  • Refreshments included

Can't make it to a scheduled workshop? We also offer private sessions for groups of 5 or more. Contact us for more information.
